Men's Bottoms Size Chart

This size chart is to determine your bottoms' size. If any of your measurement is on the borderline between two sizes, you can pick the smaller size for a tighter fit or the larger size for a looser fit. If your hip and waist measurements correspond to two different suggested sizes, you should order the one which is indicated by the measurement of your hips.
